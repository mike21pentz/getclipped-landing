How it works

Three steps
to viral clips

01

Post Your Job

Describe your content type, target platform, and style. Upload raw footage directly or link your stream VODs. Set your budget and turnaround time.

02

Match & Audition

Browse vetted clippers or run an open audition where multiple clippers submit sample clips. Pick the one whose style fits your brand perfectly.

03

Approve & Grow

Review clips in-app, request revisions, then approve with one click. Payment releases from escrow only when you're happy. Track clip performance over time.
